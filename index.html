Hello, my name is Max Asselmeier

Welcome to my website, here is a little information about me:

I am from Wheaton, Illinois which is a northwest suburb of Chicago.

I graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a BS in mechanical engineering and a minor in computer science.

I am a Robotics PhD student at Georgia Tech where I am advised by Patricio Vela and Ye Zhao.

I am investigating how to design a theoretical framework for legged navigation that can provide formal guarantees about safety, task completion, and hopefully more.

I love working with robots and going to concerts.
